## My Takeaways

<!-- What was most valuable to you personally from this session? -->



The Role of Identity in Security.



Modern Access Control: there is a strong recommendation to move away from legacy Vault Access Policies in favor of Azure RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) to align with Zero Trust principles.



Managed Identities: Using Managed Identities (specifically system-assigned) for workloads like App Services is highlighted as a best practice to securely fetch secrets from the Key Vault without manual credential handling.



Networking: Implementing Private Endpoints is crucial to keep traffic within the Azure network boundary.



Always follow the principle of Least Privilege when assigning roles.
