## Key Concepts I Learned

- Azure Policy is a governance service that helps organizations enforce standards and assess compliance across Azure resources.
- Policies can be assigned at different scopes, including management groups, subscriptions, resource groups, and individual resources.
- Policy initiatives allow multiple related policies to be grouped together and managed as a single unit.
- Azure Policy continuously evaluates resources for compliance and identifies resources that violate organizational policies.
- Remediation tasks can be used to bring existing non-compliant resources into compliance after a policy has been assigned.
- Policy exemptions can be created for resources that require temporary or permanent exclusion from specific policy requirements.
